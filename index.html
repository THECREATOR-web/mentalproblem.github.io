Anxiety

Symptoms: restlessness, tense muscles, racing thoughts. Support: paced breathing, grounding (5 things you can see), tiny steps.

Panic attack

Symptoms: sudden intense fear, chest tightness, heart racing, breathlessness, dizziness. Support: move to a safe place, slow deep breaths, name objects around you, remind yourself it will pass. Seek medical help if this is a first-time episode.

Possible heart attack

Symptoms: chest pain/pressure, pain spreading to jaw/arm/shoulder, shortness of breath, sweating, nausea. Emergency: this can be life‚Äëthreatening ‚Äî call your local emergency number immediately (e.g., 911, or your country's emergency line).

Epilepsy / seizure

Symptoms: convulsions, stiffening, loss of awareness, sudden staring, unusual movements. First aid: keep the person safe, move objects away, cushion their head, time the seizure, don't put anything in their mouth. Call emergency services if the seizure lasts >5 minutes, repeats, or it's a first seizure.

PTSD (Post‚ÄëTraumatic Stress)

Symptoms: flashbacks, nightmares, avoidance, hypervigilance, strong emotional reactions. Support: grounding techniques, predictable routines, safe people, and professional help when needed.

Stress & sleep

Symptoms: difficulty falling asleep, waking often, irritability. Support: wind-down routine, reduce screens before bed, short calming practices before sleep.
